About Me

Hi there! My name is Jasmine. I'm a software engineer with a passion for design and an artist.

I have a very vast background in the field of computer science. In 2013, I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering from the University of Colorado. Upon graduating, I worked at Western Digital as a firmware engineer for 4 years writing software for embedded devices and for test automation. I then attended University of , Riverside for a Master of Science in Computer Science from 2017-2018 in order to learn further expand my knowledge in different fields of computer science. During this time, I did research in the field of data science.

I've always had a passion for the arts but have lost sight in it when I started focusing on the sciences. In 2016, I rediscovered watercoloring and haven't stopped creating art in any medium since.

I only recently began to merge my two passions, technology and the arts, and am on a mission to utilize software to design visuals and interfaces that are beautiful.

Languages: Python, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Matlab, C/C++

Design Tools: Affinity Design, Affinity Photo, Procreate, Watercolor, Pen & Paper

Frameworks: Bootstrap
